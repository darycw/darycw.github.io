Go whale watching!

Courtesy of Seabreeze Adventures, visitors may take a day-long boat tour of the maritime areas surrounding Steveston. Interesting wildlife including orcas and seals may potentially be spotted. Safety is of paramount importance to Seabreeze, whose staff are trained professionals. You can rest assured that you will have a safe, yet fun and educational day should you book a trip with them.

Visit Garry Point Park!

Garry Point Park is a small park on the edge of Steveston. A small beach and green area brings many people to visit the park, especially during the summer months, where one can take long walks along the trail or have picnics in the sun. Biking is also possible and permitted.
