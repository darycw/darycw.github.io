Welcome to our humble shop!

Located in the heart of historic Steveston, British Columbia, Heritage Hobbies is a local small business dedicated to preserving the cultural and historical legacy of this once-bustling fishing village. Our goal is to promote awareness about the various businesses, events, and of course people who make up the identity of this community. We are also both a physical storefront as well as an online retailer of all things related to Steveston.

The History of Steveston

Founded in the 1880s by William Herbert Steves in the southwest corner of Lulu Island, the village of Steveston soon became notable during the 20th century as the site of a major salmon cannery, right at the mouth of the Fraser River. Because of the fishing industry, Steveston became home to a large, seasonal, and diverse workforce, with many Japanese, Chinese, First Nations and European fishermen and labourers working together. Today, this diversity is reflected for instance in the strong Japanese-Canadian influence within the community, as one can find numerous Japanese restaurants throughout popular tourist locations in the village. Although the salmon canning industry inevitably went into decline, and the community was absorbed into the greater city of Richmond, Steveston still remains a popular getaway for both tourists and local residents alike.
