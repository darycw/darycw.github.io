Welcome to ForesTree!

Founded in 2020, we are a local Canadian business operating in Vancouver. Our trees are sourced straight from the heart of the British Columbian interior. They come in all colours, shapes and sizes.

British Columbia is home to many different species of trees, including but not limited to pine, spruce, fir, cedar, and hemlock trees.

Our company sells a wide variety of trees.

Did you know that...

  • Estimates say that there are over 3 trillion mature trees on Earth.
  • The history of trees goes back some 370 million years.
  • Some species of trees can live for thousands of years.
